Welcome to yuso

This site is dedicated to people who want to transport or hire someone to get goods from a place to another as cheap as possible. You can get your auction published in a few clicks, or make some money by part-taking onto some auctions already created by other users.

In order to create an auction, you need to click on the 'Create auction' button above, fill out a form with some basic information and click 'Submit'. From now on transporters can take part on your auction. As an transporter, you can search for auctions or view the auctions you've already won by using the sidebar. In the same place you can also chat and find statistics. Additional news will be posted here, right on the homepage. The Yuso Team, wishes you good luck and profit!
