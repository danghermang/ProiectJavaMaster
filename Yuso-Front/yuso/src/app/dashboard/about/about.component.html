About us

Yuso is the world's most reliable an trustworthy app used to facilitate the life of both transporters and clients.

You may have a large object that needs to be moved from on side of the country to another. I guess you don't want to spend time looking through all kinds of sites for someone that may or may not be willing to help you and if they help you, they will charge lots of money from you.

You may own a big car or a truck and you want to make a living by transporting objects, but clients are hard to find and harder to keep.

The answer to all these problems is Yuso. We give you the power to choose the people you work with, we give you the control on your shipments and offer a way to earn a living.
